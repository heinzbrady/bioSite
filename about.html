Biography

Collyn Reece Heinz is a dedicated educator and coach from Haxtun, Colorado. At 27 years old, he has built a life centered around helping others grow both on and off the field. Currently working as a Physical Education teacher and Athletic Director at Haxtun Schools, Collyn is passionate about developing young athletes and guiding students to reach their full potential.

Collyn's love for sports, especially baseball, began as a way to spend time with his brother. Over time, that interest grew into a lifelong passion. Today, he spends much of his time coaching baseball, golfing, and being with his family, including his young child. His commitment to his athletes goes beyond the game, as he strives to teach them discipline, teamwork, and the importance of doing their best in everything they do.

Throughout his life, Collyn has faced significant challenges, including undergoing multiple open-heart surgeries starting at a young age. Despite these obstacles, he has remained strong, supported by his family. Collyn's goals include leading Haxtun's baseball program to a state championship while continuing to positively impact the lives of his students.
